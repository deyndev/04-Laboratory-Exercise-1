Nutritional Content

While the flesh of a banana is well-known for its nutritional benefits, the peel also contains valuable nutrients. It's a good source of fiber, potassium, and vitamins like B6 and B12.

Skincare

Banana peels can be utilized as a natural remedy for various skin issues. Rubbing the inside of a banana peel on acne-prone areas may help reduce inflammation and bacteria due to its antimicrobial properties. Additionally, the moisturizing effect of banana peel can soothe dry skin and minimize the appearance of fine lines.
